Steampunk Vibes

Engineered to Soar

Aeronautics extends Create's kinetic systems into the third dimension, letting you design, build, and pilot fully functional flying machines.

Modular Airships

Assemble your vessel block-by-block. Attach rotors, balloons, engines, and cargo holds to form a ship as unique as your vision.

Flagship Feature

Create Integration

Directly power your contraptions with Create's rotational force. Waterwheels, windmills, and steam engines all feed into your flight systems.

Wind Currents

Navigate dynamic wind systems that shift by biome and altitude. Master the currents and you'll cross entire continents without a drop of fuel.

Autopilot Circuits

Wire up redstone and Create's logic components to automate routes, altitude control, and landing procedures on your airships.

Steam Propulsion

Burn coal, wood, or blaze rods in your boiler to generate the steam pressure needed to drive massive rotary propulsion engines.

Airship Combat

Mount cannons and ballistas onto your vessel. Engage rival captains in aerial duels and defend your floating fortresses from sky pirates.

From Blueprint to the Clouds

Three steps stand between you and the open sky.

I

Forge Your Frame

Lay out your ship's skeleton using Aeronautics' new structural beams and hull blocks. The larger the frame, the more lift and cargo capacity you'll have.

II

Install the Engine

Place a boiler, fuel it, and connect rotary shafts to your propellers. Tune the RPM using Create's cogwheel ratio system for maximum efficiency.

III

Take the Helm

Right-click the Captain's Wheel to assemble the contraption and take flight. Chart your course, adjust altitude with the barometric lever, and soar.
